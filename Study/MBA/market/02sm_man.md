**Brand**. A brand is a combination of elements associated with a business, including products, experiences, logos, and values.
Essential for businesses to be deliberate in their branding efforts.
<u>Benefits of a strong brand: Trust, Recognition, Values and Lifestyle, Loyalty, Internal Consistency.</u>

[Build a Brand](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1VTyux1lCQ3U64fPqlygJb1a3Yt50Iur5/view?usp=drive_link) following **EPIC** Framework : <u>Existence, Purpose, Identity, and Connection.</u>

* **Existence**: This includes <u>the business's origin story and the problem it aims to solve</u>.
* **Purpose**: It articulates not just what the business does, but <u>why it does it, including its core values.</u>
* **Identity**: This encompasses the visual elements of the brand, such as logos, color palettes, and typography.
  * Color Psychology, Imagery and Typography.
* **Connection**: It focuses on how the brand communicates with customers, emphasizing the importance of choosing the right voice.
  * Businesses often create a **Persona**, a fictional representation of their ideal customer, to guide content creation.
