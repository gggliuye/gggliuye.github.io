## 3. Content Management & Build following

Engaging Content : <u>Educational, Inspirational, Fun</u>.
Using Storyboards to Plan Your Content.

Tips Help you go viral :
* Create "social currency" content
* Use a sensory "trigger"
* Evoke emotion
* Make your brand as public as possible
* Make your content practical
* Tell a story

Management techniques :
* Use **Content Calendar** to plan your posts
* **Repurposing Content** : taking existing content and presenting it in new formats to appeal to different audiences.
  * Examples include transforming a blog post into an infographic, video, or presentation, allowing for broader engagement.
* **Auditing Content** : involves creating a detailed list of your content and its engagement metrics, such as likes, shares, and comments.
  * Helps identify successful content, trends, and areas for improvement, allowing for data-driven content strategies.

Build a **following**:
1. invite facebook friends to follow your business page.
2. Post valuable post.
3. Use #hashtags and @mentions.
4. Hire social media influencers.
5. Create an advertising campaign.

* **Conversion** : #visits/sales/leads from social media,
